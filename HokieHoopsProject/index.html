About

Welcome to Hokie Hoops, an informative website all about Virginia Tech Men's Basketball! Here you will find virtually everything you'll need to know about the 2016-2017 season. Throughout the pages of this website, you will find the following:

  • The day, time, and location of all games
  • The 2016-2017 roster
  • All 2016-2017 coaches
  • Awesome photos of action-shots, Cassell, the fans, the student section, and more!
  • A trailer video comprised of previous highlights and current video recordings taken at home games
  • An audio recording about....?????
  • Any upcoming events associated with VT men's basketball

So go explore the website to check out all of these things - or read on below to discover more!
