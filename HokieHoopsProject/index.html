Cassell Coliseum

The home of our basketball games, among other sports, is Cassell. Construction of the building was completed in 1964, but it wasn't until 1976 that it was named Cassell Coliseum. Additions were completed in 1988, and it has around 10,000 seats in the house- the program often attracts capacity crowds. The students sitting in the student section are often known as the Cassell Guard, and play a huge role in the success of our team at home games!
